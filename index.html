Why London?

London, the city of museums

London is famous for its vast collection of over 170 museums, covering a wide range of topics, from ancient history and art to science and culture. This abundance and accessibility make London one of the world’s leading cultural capitals and a true "city of museums." In addition to the museums, the city boasts many other attractions, such as galleries, theaters, and iconic landmarks, making it a comprehensive cultural destination.

Fun fact: The British Museum has so many artifacts that if you spent one minute looking at each item, it would take over four years to see everything!

My favorite attractions in London

The Natural History Museum

Opened in 1881, features stunning architecture and over 80 million specimens, including a famous T. rex skeleton. It offers interactive exhibits and educational programs, making it a must-visit destination for those interested in the natural world.

Address:

Cromwell Rd, South Kensington, London SW7 5BD, Reino Unido

What I like about it

I appreciate the Natural History Museum for its captivating dinosaur exhibits, beautiful architecture, varied collections, and interactive displays, making it a great destination for families.

London Eye

The London Eye is a 135-meter tall observation wheel on the River Thames, opened in 2000. It has 32 glass capsules and offers panoramic views of London, becoming a popular landmark and tourist attraction.

Address:

Riverside Building, County Hall, London SE1 7PB, Reino Unido

What I like about it

At the top, the breathtaking panorama of London, including the shimmering Thames and St. Paul's Cathedral, took my breath away. For those 30 minutes, I felt like I was soaring over the city, capturing an unforgettable experience.

The Making of Harry Potter

The Warner Bros. Studio Tour London – The Making of Harry Potter is an enchanting experience for fans, letting them explore iconic sets, costumes, and the secrets behind the movie magic. It’s a magical adventure for any Harry Potter fan!

Address:

Studio Tour Dr, Leavesden, Watford WD25 7LR, Reino Unido

What I like about it

It feels like stepping straight into the world of Harry Potter! The detail is mind-blowing, making each part of the tour feel magical as it reveals the secrets behind every spellbinding scene!
