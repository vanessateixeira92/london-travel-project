Why London?

London, the city of museums

London is famous for its vast collection of over 170 museums, covering a wide range of topics, from ancient history and art to science and culture. This abundance and accessibility make London one of the world’s leading cultural capitals and a true "city of museums." In addition to the museums, the city boasts many other attractions, such as galleries, theaters, and iconic landmarks, making it a comprehensive cultural destination.

Fun fact: The British Museum has so many artifacts that if you spent one minute looking at each item, it would take over four years to see everything!
